About Mendez Transportation

Compassionate, reliable non-emergency medical transportation serving Central Florida families since 2018.

Our Story

Built on Care & Community

Mendez Transportation LLC was founded with a simple but powerful belief: every patient deserves a safe, dignified, and reliable ride to their medical appointments — regardless of their mobility or financial situation.

What started as a single vehicle serving Osceola County has grown into a fleet covering six counties across Central Florida. We have completed thousands of trips for dialysis patients, chemotherapy recipients, physical therapy clients, and countless specialist visits.

Our drivers are not just drivers — they are trained caregivers who understand the vulnerability of medical travel. Every trip we make is a commitment to the patients and families who trust us with their most important journeys.

Our Mission

To provide safe, compassionate, and reliable non-emergency medical transportation that empowers patients to access the care they need — with dignity and on time, every time.

Our Vision

To be Central Florida's most trusted NEMT provider — recognized not just for our reliability, but for the genuine care and respect we bring to every single patient interaction.

Our Values

Safety first, always. Compassion in every interaction. Reliability patients and families can count on. Professionalism that reflects the trust placed in us with each ride.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

01

Safety First

Every vehicle is inspected daily. Every driver is background-checked, CPR-certified, and trained in passenger assistance. There are no shortcuts when it comes to patient safety.

02

Compassion

We understand our passengers are often facing difficult health journeys. Our drivers are trained to be patient, kind, and sensitive to each individual's needs and comfort.

03

Reliability

A 98% on-time rate is not an accident — it's the result of careful scheduling, real-time route planning, and a team that takes every appointment seriously.

Professionalism

From clean, maintained vehicles to clear communication with patients and healthcare providers, we hold ourselves to a professional standard in everything we do.
