Our Coverage

Serving Central Florida

Mendez Transportation operates across a broad area of Central Florida, reaching patients in urban, suburban, and rural communities. Our primary service zone covers Orange, Osceola, and Seminole counties with same-day availability. Extended coverage to Polk, Lake, and Brevard counties is also available with advance scheduling.

Good to Know

Coverage Details

Same-Day Availability
Orange, Osceola, and Seminole counties have same-day service based on driver availability. Call by 8 AM for best same-day scheduling.
Don't See Your City?
If your city isn't listed, contact us — we may still be able to accommodate your trip. We evaluate requests outside our standard zone on a case-by-case basis.
Inter-County Trips
We regularly transport patients from one county to another — for example, from Kissimmee to a specialist in Orlando or from Lakeland to a hospital in Tampa.
