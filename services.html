Our Services

Safe, dignified transportation for every mobility level — ambulatory, wheelchair, and stretcher transport across Central Florida.

Service 01

Ambulatory Transport

Door-to-door sedan or SUV service for patients who can walk independently or need minimal assistance. Clean, comfortable vehicles with a driver who stays with you from pickup to drop-off and waits during your appointment.

Door-to-door pickup and drop-off
Driver waits up to 60 minutes at no extra charge
Air-conditioned, sanitized vehicle
Assistance with entering and exiting vehicle
Ideal for dialysis, chemotherapy, therapy visits

Ambulatory · Sedan / SUV
1–3 Passengers Medical Bag OK Oxygen Tank OK
Service 02

Wheelchair Accessible

Ramp and lift-equipped vans with certified wheelchair tie-down systems. Your chair stays secured for the entire journey — zero transfers needed. Our drivers are trained in proper securement and passenger assistance techniques.

Hydraulic ramp or lift-equipped van
Certified 4-point tie-down wheelchair securement
No transfers — ride in your own chair
Trained in power wheelchair and scooter transport
Extra caregiver/companion seat available
Book This Service
Wheelchair · Accessible Van
Power & Manual Tie-Down Certified Lift Equipped
Service 03

Stretcher Transport

Specialty transport for patients who must remain in a lying-down position throughout the journey. Our padded stretcher vehicles are equipped with safety restraints and are staffed by a trained attendant for the entire trip.

Padded stretcher with full-body restraint system
Trained attendant accompanies every trip
Climate-controlled vehicle for patient comfort
Ideal for post-surgery, fracture, or bedridden patients
Coordination with nursing home / hospital staff

Coverage

Insurance & Payment

We work with most major insurance providers and Medicaid plans in the state of Florida. Contact us to verify your coverage before your first ride.

Florida Medicaid
We are a registered NEMT provider. Medicaid-eligible trips may be covered at no cost to the patient.
Medicare Advantage
Many Medicare Advantage plans include transportation benefits. We'll verify your specific plan coverage.
Private Insurance
We accept most major private insurance plans. Pre-authorization may be required — we handle the paperwork.
Managed Care Plans
Enrolled in a managed care organization (MCO)? We work with most Florida MCOs that include NEMT benefits.
Workers' Compensation
Transportation to and from treatment facilities may be covered under workers' comp. We handle billing directly.
Private Pay
No insurance? No problem. We offer competitive private-pay rates. Contact us for a quote.

Not sure if your plan covers NEMT? Contact us and we'll verify your coverage for free.
